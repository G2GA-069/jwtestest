About

Content that resonates,
stories that stay

London-based content creator and professional model with a sharp eye for skincare, beauty, and lifestyle storytelling. Colgate University graduate (Economics & Psychology), bilingual in English and Mandarin, with an eye shaped by time at UCL and the LSE.

My modelling portfolio includes campaigns for Charlotte Tilbury, Lancôme, Le Chameau, and Canon. On the content side, I create everything from curated K-beauty campaigns to authentic UGC, always intentional, always elevated.

When I'm not in front of the camera, you'll find me deep in a skincare routine, lost in a bookshop, or planning the next trip somewhere warm.
